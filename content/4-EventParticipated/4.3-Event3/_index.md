### Overview

**FCAJ Community Day** June 2026 is the annual event of the FCAJ community, focusing on practical solutions for integrating AI into system operations, infrastructure optimization, and data security at enterprise scale. This is an opportunity for professionals, students, and technology enthusiasts to learn, exchange ideas, and connect.

This year's event featured many compelling presentations from industry-leading experts, ranging from building Voice AI Assistants for enterprises, DevOps automation, to applying Amazon Q in human resources management and securing AI systems.

### 1. Steve Tran - Cloud Infrastructure Operations and the Agentic Platform Era

**About the speaker:** Steve Tran is known as a thought leader in cloud computing, with many years of experience in building and operating cloud systems.

**Background and journey:** Steve shared his learning journey from traditional networking, transitioning to Azure and eventually to Cloud. The starting point for this journey was when he encountered continuous server failures - a challenging problem that many in the industry have also faced.

**Key question for the future:** With AI increasingly developing, Steve raised the question: **"Cloud Engineering or Developer?"** - A question that many young people are pondering when pursuing a career in technology.

**Agentic Platform - Solution for 4 Important Problems:**

The Agentic Platform that Steve introduced can handle 4 main challenges:

1. **Investigate the Incident:** Automatically analyze and investigate system incidents, significantly reducing incident resolution time.

2. **Code Review:** Support automated code checking, detect errors, and suggest improvements before merging.

3. **FinOps:** Intelligently manage cloud costs, optimize resource usage, and reduce unnecessary expenses.

4. **Security:** Automatically monitor and protect systems, detect security threats as early as possible.

### 2. Hieu Nghi, Kiet, and Trung - Voice AI Assistant for Enterprise

**About the team:** This is a group of 3 young members working together to research and build Voice AI Assistant for enterprises.

**Impressive demo:** Anh Hieu conducted an impressive demo by asking AI about MacBook. The conversation went smoothly, with AI having the ability to understand questions and respond naturally.

**Highlights of Voice AI:**

- **Low latency:** Fast response time, nearly instantaneous, creating a natural conversation experience
- **Detailed and accurate responses:** AI not only answers the core of the question but also provides additional relevant information
- **English language:** The system works well with English, demonstrating high language processing capability
- **Handles random issues:** Ability to handle unexpected questions from users flexibly

**Areas for improvement:**

- **Speech-to-Speech doesn't support Vietnamese:** This is a major limitation when deploying in the Vietnamese market. Converting speech to text and vice versa with Vietnamese still has many challenges.

**Lessons learned:**

Through this presentation, I realized there are many ways to leverage AI in daily work. Voice AI is not just about answering questions but can also be integrated into business processes, customer support, and many other applications.

### 3. Cloud Kinetics - DevOps AI Agent on AWS

**About the company:** Cloud Kinetics is a company specializing in AWS solutions in Vietnam.

**Real-world DevOps challenges:**

The team identified 4 persistent challenges that DevOps teams often face:

1. **Customer trigger:** Continuous customer requirements put great pressure on the team
2. **Fragment telemetry:** Scattered telemetry data, difficult to aggregate and analyze
3. **Knowledge gaps:** Lack of expertise in various areas
4. **Constant interruption:** Continuous disruption from incidents and new requirements

**DevOps AI Agent solution:**

Cloud Kinetics built an AWS DevOps AI Agent using:

- **MCP (Model Context Protocol):** A protocol that helps connect AI with various tools and systems
- **AI permissions spec:** Clearly defining AI agent's permissions and scope of operations

### 4. Anh Truong and Chi Minh Anh (Noventic) - Amazon Q for HR

**About the company:** Noventic is a company specializing in automation and AI solutions for enterprises.

**HR challenges being faced:**

The presentation highlighted major challenges that HR departments often encounter:

1. **Missing talents:** Too many resumes lead to insufficient evaluation, missing out on excellent candidates
2. **Resume screening and classification:** Spending lots of time reading and classifying hundreds or thousands of CVs
3. **Cybersecurity risks:** Storing candidate data on unsafe platforms
4. **New employee management:** Tracking onboarding process and evaluating new employees

**Amazon Q product demo:**

Anh Truong and Chi Minh Anh demoed the product with features:

- **Generate personalized interview questions:** Create interview questions personalized for each candidate based on CV and job requirements
- **JD creation and screening support:** Automatically create Job Description and screen suitable candidates
- **Management dashboard:** Track newly hired personnel, record evaluations in the system
- **Save offer levels:** Manage salary and offer information for each candidate
- **Export comparison files:** Generate comparison reports between candidates to support hiring decisions

### 5. Anh Toan Nguyen and Anh Hieu Nghi (Renova Cloud) - Amazon Q and MCP Server

**Problem:** How to connect Amazon Q to MCP to a third party without going through the internet?

With the need to deploy Amazon Q in enterprise environments, the team proposed a solution:

- **VPC Connection:** Secure private connection for Amazon Q
- **Architecture:** Using EC2, ALB (Application Load Balancer), Route 53 to build multi-layered security systems
- **Zero Trust:** Applying Zero Trust security model, trusting no one by default
